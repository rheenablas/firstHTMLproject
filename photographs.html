Introduction

My love for photography started when I moved in America. We were at a beach and my aunt asked me to take pictures. Instantly the camera and I clicked. I began to take pictures at events, not as a professional but for fun. My dad bought a DSLR (digital single-lens reflex) camera for the “family” but I ended up using it the most.

I don't think I've gotten the hang of using photo-editors since it takes a while. But I do enjoy taking good quality photos whenever to reminisce the sweet memories of life. I started volunteering taking photographs at school events in the Philippines.

Despite loving taking photographs of people, me on the other hand am very camera-shy. Here are rare photos of me:

Hearts

Do you believe in coincidences? Like seeing a sign/symbol everywhere? Well, ever since I was 13-14 years old, I've been seing hearts everywhere! I see it on the ground, on the back of my spoon, in the clouds. Literally everywhere! It's not like I'm searching for hearts. I just notice it.

I thought someone cursed me to see hearts everywhere, but I don't see that as harmful. Now I look back at it, I could only hope that it was a reminder that I was loved, that someone from above is looking out for me. (1 John 4:16)

Nature

I enjoy watching sunrise and sunsets. I love taking photos of nature even if I hardly go back to look at it. It reminds me of God as the creator of it all (Ephesians 3:9). His eye for details and how He formed everything through His words just makes me at awe of Him (Genesis 1:3).

People

I absolutely love taking pictures of people. I enjoy capturing their smiles, their hardwork, and just reminding them that they are beautiful (Psalm 139:14).
