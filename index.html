Differences About Schools

Schedule

Philippines is the only place where I saw students stay in one place while teachers move around.

Philippines

Time: 7:30-4:00; 1hour lunch; 2-15min break.

America

Time: 7:00-2:00; 45min lunch.

Ireland

Time: 9:00-4:00; 1-15min break; 45min lunch.

Subjects

Subjects are similar where they have a common ground of English, Maths and Science.

The curriculum in the Philippines is set where students pick a strand they want from: ABM (Accountancy, Business and Management), STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths), HUMMS (Humanities and Social Sciences), TechVoc (Technical-Vocational Livelihood). Unlike in Ireland where students are free to pick the subjects in the strands available in specific schools. It’s the same in America, where students have a choice on their subjects as long as they meet a requirement (differs per state).

Exams
Philippines

Most colleges/universities in the Philippines require an entrance exam/admission test. Entrance exam varies in difficulty, length and subject, majority a multiple choice exam or essay writing. The subjects consists of English, Maths, Science and Filipino. Students would need to apply to each school they want to apply for.

America

America have SAT (Scholastic Aptitude Test) which can be taken on junior/senior year in high school. The students apply for which college/university they want to apply to individually.

Ireland

Ireland have the Leaving Certificate exams on the summer of final year in secondary school. Students then need to create a CAO (Central Admissions Office) application to apply to Irish universities. CAO handles which college/university students would like to apply for.
